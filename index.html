Our House Blend exhibits the strength of a smooth, high-altitude Arabica, along with the boldness of a low-altitude Robusta, with low acidity. The House Blend is dark roasted (Espresso Roast), but the coffee beans themselves are not oily. Flavor notes of Tobacco, Dark Cocoa, and Almond Nut are enjoyed in a Long Black/Americano, while the notes of Dark Cocoa, Almond Nut, and Tobacco are savored in a Latte.
